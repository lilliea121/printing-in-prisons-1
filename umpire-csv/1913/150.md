Hard hitting, sharp fielding, a brilliant catch, or glaring error, will put your name in the ‘‘who’s who—and which’ column. A knock today may be a boost tomorrow. It all depends on individual playing. ‘Teamwork deserves notice. Batteries are always closely watched. The world loves a winner, and a loser is soon forgotten. Papers made much of the fact that Roosevelt and Jeffries could not ‘‘come back.’’    Grandstandism is tabooed. Play the game; it’s up to you.
