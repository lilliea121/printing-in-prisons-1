The managers of baseball, cricket, quoits, hockey, marbles, catty, and several other kinds of teams, have all missed a glorious opportunity to sign up a first-class all-around athlete, when for the past two seasons they have overlooked that prodigy connected with the mechanical department of the UMPIRE, and favorably known as Joe. The appearance of the yards these days recalls the old song, “Every day is Sunday.”
