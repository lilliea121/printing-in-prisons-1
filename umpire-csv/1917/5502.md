Religious Services   Catholic Mass is celebrated at the Prison Chapel every Sunday at 7.30 o'clock, A.M. Confession every Saturday morning.      General services are held on the various Blocks at 9 o'clock every Sunday morning.      Episcopal services are held at the Chapel on the third Thursday of every month.      Jewish services are held on Saturdays at the band-room.
