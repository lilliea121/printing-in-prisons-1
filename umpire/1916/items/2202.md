Episcopal Services at the Chapel

Episcopal services were held at the Chapel on Thursday morning last at 10 A. M.

The sacrament was administered to sixty-two inmate communicants by the Rev. J. T. Taylor, the Rev. Mr. Schenestry assisting.

Confirmation services were held in the afternoon, at which time twenty inmates were presented to Bishop Garland for confirmation.

There were present Mrs. Garland, Miss M. Coates, Miss Dupree, Mrs. Reed, Mrs. Bartlett, Mrs. Keen, and Dr. William H. Jef- freys. the newly appointed superintendent of the P. E. City Mission.
