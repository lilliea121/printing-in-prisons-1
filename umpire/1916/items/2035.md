“There are many who are esteemed by the world and accounted among its elect who, by process of suppression, have learned to conform strictly to the conventions of life, and do not fail in any test of conduct. But is conduct all? Is it not the least? Are not thoughts things? What was meant when it was said, “As a man thinketh in his heart so is he’’? If in our hearts we plan evil of our brother and yet repress the physical, are we guiltless? Surely not.”
