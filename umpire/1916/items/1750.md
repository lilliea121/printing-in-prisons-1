Baseball Games of the Week

Tuesday’s Games Printers—2 Cubs—3.

In a hotly contested game, the Cubs, won from the Printers, by the score of 3—2.

Mac., on the mound for the Printes, pitched a good game, but several errors and a wild throw to 2nd base, put him at disadvantage, and gave the Cubs the lead.

Mac. allowed 5 hits, walked 2 and struck out 6.

Albie, was touched up for 7 hits, five of these being doubles, and had 3 strike outs.

Score by innings as follow:
R H E.
Printers 0 1 0 0 0 1 0 0 0—2 7 1
Cubs 0 2 1 0 0 0 0 0 0—3 5 0

Doubles—Stump, Slats, Fingers, Hagy, Jack and Mooney.

Umpires—Marty and Happy.

Wednesday’s Games 

Printers—1 Ninth4.

Timely hitting coupled with good fielding gave the Ninth a victory over the Printers, by the score of 4—1.

The Printers, lone tally as scored by Smith, in the 5th inning, when he connected with one of Spooky’s shoots for a circuit drive.

Spooky, on the mound for the Ninth, allowed only two hits, but was wild at times and walked 5 men, and struck out 8.

Hagy, for the Printers, allowed 7 hits, walked 3, and struck out 7.

Ninth 1 0 0 0 0 3 0 0 0—4 7 0

Doubles——Jacoby and Chick. 
Home run—Smith.
