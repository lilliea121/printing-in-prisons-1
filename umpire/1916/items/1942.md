THE MEANING OF MISFORTUNE

As a father in a garden stoops down to kiss a child the shadows of his body fall down upon it; so many of the dark misfortunes of our life are not God going away from us, but our Heavenly Father stooping down to give us the kiss of His infinite and everlasting love.
