H.F. C.—15 Blocks—15

In aloosely played game the Blocks won a victory over the H. F. C. by the score of 21 — 15.

Slats pitched for the Blocks, and allowed 17 hits. struck out 8, and walked 5.

Maek twirled for the H. F. C. and was found for 27 hits, walking 3 and striking out 10.
