CAPITOL
INTO RIVER

According to advices received here from La
Paz, capitol of Bolivia, a section of the city
about 1600 vards long, is sliding into the
Choqueyapu River. The section comprises
mostly houses of the poor, although there are
markets, barracks and the museum of the
archaeologist, Posnansky.
