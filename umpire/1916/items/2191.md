While passing through this institution, in company with others, a fair maid was heard to remark, “Oh, isn’t he nice looking?’’ At which her friend replied, “Why, they're both good lvokers.” Just then Overseers Loan and Moore hove in sight. 'Nough said. Please pass the mustard.
