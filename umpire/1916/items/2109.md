U. S. Troops to be Withdrawn from Mexico

It is reported that the American-Mexican joint commission reachedan agreement to the effect that the United States would withdraw the expeditionary column under General Pershing within the forty days after the formal indorsement of the agreementby President Wilson and General Carranza, provided conditions in northern Mexico are such as to warrant such action.
