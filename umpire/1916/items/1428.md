The strength of organized labor and its steady gain was evidenced at the recent session of the State Federation of Labor at Beaver Falls, Pa. When President James M. Maurer, of Reading, read the annual report, it showed that in the year just closed organized labor has made more progress in strengthening organizations and obtaining legislation than has ever been accomplished in any two years since its existence.
