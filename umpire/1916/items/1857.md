LABOR DAY CELEBRATION BY E.S. P. BAND

Labor Day was fittingly observed at this ti 129 institution by a band concert under the able direction of Prof. A. F Wiegandt. 

The programme, which is printed below, was specially selected for the occasion, and its clever rendition by the E. S. P. Band elicited much praise.

PROGRAMME
Entry March   Hall
"New Colonial"
"America"
1 March   Doring
"Distant Greeting"
2 Fantasia   Wallace
3 Waltz   "Skaters"  Waldeufel
4 Serenade   "Amina"   Lincke
INTERMISSION
5 Selection   Verdi
6 Patrol   "Blue and Gray"   Cox
7 Selection   Verdi
8 Rag   "Two Key"   Hollander
9 March   "King Karl"   Unrath
"Star Spangled Banner"
Exit March   Rosaukasa
"Chief of Staff"
