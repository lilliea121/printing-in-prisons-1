Honor Club 10—Blocks 2

The Block team after being re-enforced by several stars, were unable to cope with the Honor Club, who won by the score of 10 to 2.

Charley, pitching for the H. F. C.,, allowed 10 hits, walked 5 and struck out 3.

Silly, for the Blocks, allowed 16 hits, walked 3 and failed to have a strike out.

One of the features of the game was a home run drive by Taylor. 
Umpire—Jacoby.
