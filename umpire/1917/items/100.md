His Case Spoiled by Hen’s Cackle

‘‘Betrayed by a Cackle”’ would have been a fitting title for a scene enacted in the court at West Chester, Pa. Recently Lewis A. Saddler was on trial, charged with the theft of some chickens from Mrs. Martha Wash- ington. Saddler had a fair case until one of the hens was brought into court and greeted its owner with a cackle of effusive recognition.
