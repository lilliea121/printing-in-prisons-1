RESPITE GRANTED FOR FIFTH TIME

It was announced on Saturday last that Governor Brumbaugh had issued a respite staying the electrocution of Henry Ward Mottern, of Jefferson County, from the week of October 22 to the week of October 29, in order to permit an application for a rehearing of the case to be made. This makesthe fifth respite granted to Mottern. His counsel hopes to save him from the chair.
