The city of Roanoke is scarcely more than twenty-five years old, but it has a population of about 30,000, and it is sometimes called the Chicago of Virginia. It was produced by the location of the Norfolk & Western shops. Men who were failures elsewhere have made their fortunes in Roanoke,
