ODE TO MR. MOUSE

I wake in the night, 
I hear a noise so queer. 
It’s the little gray mouse 
That most females fear. 
I lay quietly down, 
But around the room peep, 
And hear the sound of gnawing paper 
As the penitentiary sleeps. 

—B 8312.
