NEW TELEPHONE RECEIVER

An improved telephone receiver has been put on the market by the use of which it is said that even persons partially deaf can hear over the telephone. The receiver has a trumpet shaped design, with a fluted earpiece, and is so made as to shut out all external noises. It is also claimed for it that it does away with the ear-splitting cracks which result when central is working to get a party who ‘‘doesn’t answer.’’
