Pitisburg Talks to Nauen, Germany

Wireless telegraph operators at the Car- negie Technological Institute talked with the German stations at Nauen and Eilvese Satur- day. The distance from Nauen to Pittsburg

is 3980 miles. Messages passing between wireless statiorns in the Central Empires re- lating to troop movements and diplomatic conversation were heard. ;
