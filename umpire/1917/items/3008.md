LABOR DAY CELEBRATION BY E. S. P. BAND

Labor Day was fittingly observed at this institution by a band concert under the able direction of Prof. A. F. Wiegandt.

PROGRAM 
Entry March Marine Flashes Thome 
“America’’
1. Aida March Verdi
2. Show Bell Schlepegreli
3. Sextette, from Lucia St. Clair
4. Martha Selection St. Clair
5. Il Trovatore Selection Sofranck
6. Quartette, from Rigoletto Verdi
7. Dope Rag Powell
8. Flag of Victory March Blon 
‘‘Star Spangled Banner’’ 
Exit March Minerskelly Greenfield
