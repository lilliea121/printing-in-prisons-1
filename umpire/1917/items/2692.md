MEMORIAL DAY TO BE OBSERVED BY BAND CONCERT

Memorial day will be fittingly observed at this institution by a band concert on the Center gandt has arranged a selection at 2:30 P. M. Prof A. F. Weigandt has arranged a selection of patriotic and popular music for rendition by the E. S. P. band on this occassion and it is anticipated that an enjoyable afternoon for the inmates will follow. The program as arranged is as follows:

PROGRAM

Entry March “National Emblem’’ Bagley 
“America’’

1. “The Honor & Friendship Club March” B 8099
2. “Strong and True” Overture Kiesler
3. “Golden Showers’’ Waltz Hall
4. “The Jazz” Fox Trot Baskette 
5. "Fest" Overture Leutner
6. ‘‘Patrol Comique’’ Hindey .
7. ‘‘Andante Religioso” Thome
8. “Song of the Nation”’ Lampe 
Exit March “The Navigator’’ Rosenkrans
