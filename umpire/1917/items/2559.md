Oldest Ball-player Dead

It is announced by a local paper at Odgen, Utah, that Edward (Eddie) Miller, one of the oldest professicnal baseball players in the United States, and the oldest dispatcher in point of service of the entire Southern Pacific Railroad system, died there recently. Miller was a member of the Boston and Brooklyn baseball teams before the National League was organized. He was a teammate of Sidney Farrar, of the old Hub City team. He was born in Toronto, Canada, 67 years ago.
