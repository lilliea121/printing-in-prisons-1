Erratum

In an article on Christmas decorations in our last issue, credit was given inmate B 8361 for painting the shadow-box picture, ‘“The Three Wise Men.”’ Correction is mod- estly requested by him, which we are glad to accord the skilful artist inmate B 7648, to whom the credit is due.Erratum
