NOTICE

The Engineering Courses which have been conducted in the E. S. P. during the past two years by the State College and the Y. M. C. A. will be continued. New classes will start shortly. Mechanical Drawing, Shop Mathematics, elementary as well as advanced taken up. Any one interested can send a note through his Overseer to Mr. Bonsall, and he will be given an interview.
