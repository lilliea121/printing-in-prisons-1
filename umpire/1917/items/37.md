NOTICE

After an application for reparole or a re- hearing hus been heard by the Committee of the Board of Inspectors and Refused, the Committee will not consider any new appli- cation in the case within six (6) months from the date of such refusal.

In all letters to the Visiting Inspectors, you must state the full reasons for any inter- view requested.

BY ORDER OF BOARD OF INSPECTORS.
