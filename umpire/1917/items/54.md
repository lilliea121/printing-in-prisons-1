Sunday Called “Greatest Showman”

When the Associaticn of the Gutdoor Show- men of the World was organized a few weeks ago «Billy’’ Sunday was extended a cordial invitation to be present. In his letter of in- vitation Frank P. Spellman said: ‘“We would like to have you with us, for we believe you are a greater showman than any of us.”'
