JEWISH INMATES APPRECIATE GIFT

The Jewish inmates of this institution are desirous of expressing their thanks and appreciation to Mr. Abraham Rosenberg, of this city, for his kindness in donating a number of useful books for their use and edification.

Mr. Frank F. Eiseman of 816 No. 8th street, this city, visited this institution on Saturday last and gave each Jewish inmate a copy of the Bible, for which the undersigned desires to express thanks and appreciation.
