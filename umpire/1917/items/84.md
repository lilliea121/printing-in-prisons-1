Asks Jail Sentence as Drink Cure

Believing that he could not stop drinking in any other way, W. E. Brown, of Echo, Ore., begged the Portland District Attorney to put him in jail. “I’ve tried for twelve years and can’t let the booze alone,’” he said. He was sent to the county jail pending a de- cision as to the law in his case.
