ENGLISH “DOLLAR BILLS”

A London, Eng., reviewer reports that the agitation for the issue of five shilling currency notes appears to be reviving. The scarcity in the commercial supply of bar silver is touched upon, and the question is said to have arisen whether the Government might not by the substitution of paper for silver coin realize a considerable profit for the Exchequer by selling the surplus metal for use where it is much more wanted.
