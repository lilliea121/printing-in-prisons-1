Religious Services at the E. S. P. Chapel

On Thursday morning last Episcopal ser- vices were held at the prison chapel, the Rev. Joks Faylor. officiating. Communion was administered to the inmate members of the church and some visitors who were present.

Confirmation instruction was given to a large class in the afternoon, an edifying sermon on ‘‘Spiritual Rewards’’ following.Religious Services at the E. S. P. Chapel
