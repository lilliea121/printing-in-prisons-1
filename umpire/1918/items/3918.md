COMMONWEALTH OF PENNSYLVANIA 
EXECUTIVE CHAMBER 
HARRISBURG PA.

Rev. Joseph Welch, 
Moral Instructor,

My Dear Mr. Welch: 
I wish you would convey to the men my sincere congratulations and thanks for their wonderful support of the War Chest movement. It is a splendid and altogether praiseworthy accomplishment.

With sincere regards to all, I am, 
Very Truly Yours, 
M. G. BRUMBAUGH.
