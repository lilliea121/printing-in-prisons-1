ALCOHOL THE CHIEF CAUSE OF CRIME Liquor was responsible during 1916 for the greatest crime wave in the history of Harrisburg Pa.; according to the report of the Superintendent of Detectives, William L. Windsor. The arrests 3,857, nearly 1,000 more than in 1915. Nearly every one of the men and women arrested were either drunk or to some degree under the influence of alcohol.
