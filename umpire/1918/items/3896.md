HONOR CLUB NOTES

At the Delegate meeting on Tuesday May 21st. 1918 the following were admitted to membership in the Club: B 9188—$1.00; B 9190 25c; 9187, 9184, 9193, 9192, 9194, 9185 9189, 9162, 9178, 9195, 8038, 9191,

Treasurers report showed the same Balance as the proceeding week $45.55. The Expenditures of the week were: to Mrs. Bartlett of the King’s Daughters, in partial re-imbursement of money expended in aiding the family of an Inmate $1.00; to John Wanamaker for 20 yards of Khaki cloth $10.00; Loaned to B 6605, $15.00, this Loan secured by P. L. Commission Credit; loaned to B 7272 $1.25; Donation to War Chest Fund $10.00; for purchase of War Savings Stamps $1.00;o Frank C. Kenyon for U. S. Flag $6,50, this covered by collection; to Chaplain for 12 spools of Khaki thread 60c. Receipts, from circulating Library $7.75; Donations $1.25; Collection for Flag on 5th. Gallery $6.50; Collection for Orchestra Music $13.23.
