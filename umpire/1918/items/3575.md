THE RARE QUALITY

To be able to summon courage enough to acknowledge one’s own wrong-doing is to be possessed of qualities that raise humanity to the plane of the angels, observes a writer.

The man who has this courage in the highest degree ‘‘cannot in the world be singly counterpoised.”
