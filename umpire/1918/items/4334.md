The E.S.P. ‘“Volunteers’’ in recognition of his splendid work in bringing the ‘“Army’’ safely intact thru thelate siege of influenza, have been pleased to confer the Honorary Grade of Surgeon General, upon Dr. H W. Hassell, Resident Physician of the Institution and latest reports have it that the recipient is wearing his laurels full modestly. No member of the Volunteers will row dare to be ill; for he will be court-martialed who’d refuse to take a pill,
