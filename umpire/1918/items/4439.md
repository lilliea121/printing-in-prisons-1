President Wilson will head the American delegation to the Peace Conference. The delegation will consist of the President; Robert Lansing, Secretary of State; Henry White, former Ambassador to France, and Italy; Col. E. N. House; Gen. Tasker H. Bliss, representative of the American army in the Supreme War Council.
