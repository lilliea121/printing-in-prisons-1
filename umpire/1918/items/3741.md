Capt. Parker’s Squad Wins

Capt. Parker’s team defeated Capt. Dutton’s team after a very interesting game by the close score of 10—9. The feature of the game was the coaching of Capt. Parker and home runs by Jas. M. Caughan and Jno. McLaughlin, and the catching of Billy Andrews.
