RELIGIOUS SERVICES

Catholic Mass is celebrafed at the Prison Chapel every Sunday at 7.30 o’clock, A. M.

Confession every Saturday morning.

General serviees are held on the various Blocks at 9 o’clock every Sunday morning.

Episcopal services are held at the Chapel on the third Thursday of every month. 

Jewish services are held on Saturdays at the band-room.
