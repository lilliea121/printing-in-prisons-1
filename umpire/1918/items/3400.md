CAN'T HOLD OUT MUCH LONGER

Many people think we are having a pretty serious time over the food and coal situation is this country at the present time, but according to the Baltimore American, only one egg is allowed an individual in Berlin every three weeks. Coal is sold by the scoopfuls, and, with the winter weather, official efficiency will be greatly taxed to keep up enthusiasm in the war for a much longer period.
