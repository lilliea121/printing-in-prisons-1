NEW NITRATE PLANT

One of the nitrate plants for which Congress appropriated $20,000,000, willbe located at Sheffield, Ala., according to reports from the war department. The place chosen is on the Tennessee river near Muscle Shoals. This plant will manufacture ammonia and nitric acid.
