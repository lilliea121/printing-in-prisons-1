RELIGIOUS SERVICES

Catholic Mass is celebrated at the Prison Chapel every Sunday at 7.30 o’clock, A. M. Confession every Saturday morning.

General services are held on the various Blocks at 9 o’clock every Sunday morning.

Episcopal services for men, Eastern Penitentiary. 3rd Thursday each month, 9.30 A. M., 1st & 3rd Thursdays each month 2 P. M.

Jewish services are held on Saturdays at the band-room.
