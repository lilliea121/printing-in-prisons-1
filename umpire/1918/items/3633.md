COUNTERFEITING TOO COSTLY

Counterfeiters are having a hard time of it these days, as was recently divulged, owing to war time prices making metals so costly that all first-class sperious coin makers are going out of the business. That is the explanation of United States Treasury Department men accounting for the marked decrease in counterfeiting since war begun.
