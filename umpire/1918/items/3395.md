Thomas A. Fairbrother, an officer at this institution for the past twenty-seven years and who resided at 2417 W. Allegheny avenue, this city, died at his residence on Friday morning last. He was a member of Robert Morris Council, No. 40, O. I. A., and a member of St. James P. E. Church. His funeral will take place at the above named church on Wednesday at 2.45 P. M. He leaves a widow and a number of friends to mourn his loss.

The men of the 11th Block, who have but kind memories of the late Mr. Fairbrother, opened wide their purse strings, and purchashed a large floral piece to be sent his family.
