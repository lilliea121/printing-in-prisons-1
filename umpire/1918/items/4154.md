SING SING CONTRIBUTES

A war savings stamp campaign just concluded by inmates of Sing Sing prison added $1,000 to Uncle Sam’s war chest. The prisoners subscribed to several thousand dollars’ worth of Liberty Bonds during the third drive and contributed $1,000 in the last Red Cross campaign.
