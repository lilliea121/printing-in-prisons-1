MAKE GOOD FIGHTERS

In a recent and widely copied newspaper article prisoners were held to be disqualified as fighters by reason of their lack of courage.

"The German Army would like nothing better than to face regiments of convicts," was the conclusion reached in the article.

The following figures cover the record of 7,000 men released from English prisons to fight for their country:

Killed In Action 550 Died of wounds 49 Died of Sickness 31

More or less Seriously Wounded 1,350 - Awarded Distinguished Conduct Medals

25 Mentioned In Dispatches 20 Commissioned 8 Awarded the Victoria Cross 3

: (The highest honor England can confer on her soldiers and sailors.)

A total of 593 menregistered on Thursday, not a "conscientious objector" among them. every man ready to "work or fight."
