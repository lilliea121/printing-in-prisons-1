Some of the supplies that the Y. M. C. A. has recently shipped to France show the extent and nature of its service to the boys overseas: 5,000,000 bottles of "pop," 10,000,000 sheets of writing paper, 6,000,000 envelopes, 2,500,000 packages of chewing gum, 200 motion-picture machines, 100 phonographs, 2,500 phonograph records, 200,000 Testaments, $100,000 worth of athletic supplies and great quantities of toilet articles and food.
