WHO'S WHO In MacKentieville.

Who is the man who mends our clock, and has a mortgage on the Seventh block? Who’ll take a watch or clock apart, just

to show you, he is some smart? Who sometimes sends it back to you, minus just a spring or wheel or two? Who

when you ask, where are those wheels, just simply smiles and turns on his heels ? Who fixes ’em all, no matter the make? Why that gent from Chermany, dot Herr Dutch Jake. — Spider.
