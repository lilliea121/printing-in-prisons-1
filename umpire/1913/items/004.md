The “Dutch” failed to String the “Harps”

A Team composed of Irish, and one
of Dutch, played a game at Southern
Pines on 8t. Patrick’s Day. The Irish
'won by the Score of 7 to 6,

In the 8th and 9th inning, Seaton for
the ‘‘Harps’’, fanned 5 straight men.
Chalmers was on the firing line for the
Dutchmen and was touched up for 9 hits.

The regular team plays Baltimore, of
the International League, at Fayettville,
N. C. today.
