PITHY SAYINGS BY MEN OF
EMINENCE

Make the most of yourself, for that is
all there is of you.— Ralph Waldo Emerson.

Adapt thyself to the things with which
thy lot has been cast.— Marcus Awurelius.

There is only—gr_le— ley to be happy,

and that is to make somebody else so.—
Sid. Smith.
