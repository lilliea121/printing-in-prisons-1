Music has charms,
Farmers have barns,
Liars have yarns,
Pitchers have arms.
The table has legs, but cannot walk.
The cave has a mouth, but cannot talk.
So what’s the difference, we’ll all see
stars some day, but don’t get hit with
the ball. Leave it to the umpire.

— Jingles.
