Poet, to his wife, awakening at mid-
night with an inspiration.—‘‘Git up,
quick, D’ve just thought of a good word.”’
““Oh, go to sleep,’”’ replied his wife petu-
lantly, I’ve just thought of a mighty bad
word.”’
