The ‘‘Rag-a-time-a- Wop’’ wanta know
why every time start-a dat syncopation
motion, every bod shaka demselves up.
And when hear dat Overture of William
Tell, every one maka dat br-rrrr, special
da Band men.—A4ngelo.
